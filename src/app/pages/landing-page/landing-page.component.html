How MediTech Works

Our secure platform combines blockchain technology with IPFS distributed storage to keep your medical records safe, accessible, and tamper-proof.

Secure Data Storage

Medical records are encrypted and stored on IPFS, a distributed file system that ensures your data is secure and always available.

Blockchain Verification

Every record is verified on the blockchain, creating an immutable audit trail that guarantees data integrity and authenticity.

Controlled Access

You control who can access your medical data. Grant and revoke permissions to healthcare providers with confidence.
